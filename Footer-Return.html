RETURN & EXCHANGE




Can I do an exchange if the sizing/fitting don't fit me?

We do not exchange but we accept return. You may return your item and store credit will be refunded for your next purchase. You may use it to purchased your preferred item.

We only accept returns for items that are in its original condition (with tag attached, unworn, unwashed and unaltered). You will be issued a store credit equivalent to the purchase price of each returned item(s). Delivery fees to return your order, if any, will not be refunded.


*Purchase of sale items( eg : Mother's day codes, Xmas codes and other special codes), intimates, accessories, bag and specifically stated non-returnable items are final and cannot be returned.

*A processing fee of RM6 (WM) & RM10 (EM) will be deducted from your store credits if free shipping had been provided for the order.
