About Us



HERME3 Enterprise

HERME3 is a fashion brand established in Malaysia. With our passion for fashion and our goal of bringing affordable products to our customers, we have grown rapidly through our customer-centric approach and our commitment to introducing fast fashion trends at affordable prices without compromising on quality.


Our brand believes that everyone can express confidence through the way they dress.

Our aim is to inspire this belief in personal style by dressing men/women with our products that are inspired by our customers.


In addition to having a keen eye for ever-changing fashion trends, we also believe in creating pieces that are exciting in style.

We're constantly pushing the boundaries, reinventing new styles that showcase our charming charm and fun personalities.

All clothing on HERME3 is self-manufactured.
